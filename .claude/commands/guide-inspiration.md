# Guide Inspiration — Cozy Gaming Setups

Generate fresh setup guide ideas by matching current aesthetic trends to the store's concept. The output is a shortlist of concrete, actionable guide ideas — each with a vibe, an anchor product, a product list, and Pinterest keyword potential.

## What this skill does

It reasons through:
1. What aesthetics are currently trending on Pinterest and TikTok
2. Which of those trends are a good fit for the cozy / feminine / aesthetic gaming niche
3. What gap exists in the current guide lineup
4. What products on Amazon would build a complete, shoppable guide

## Step 1 — Know the existing guides (never duplicate)

Current published guides:
- My ultimate pink cozy gaming setup → /pink-cozy-gaming-setup/
- Pink flower gaming setup inspiration → /pink-flower-gaming-setup/
- Minimalist white battlestation → /minimalist-white-battlestation/
- Pastel cozy coding & gaming desk setup → /pastel-coding-gaming-desk/

A new guide must have a meaningfully different aesthetic, anchor product, or mood. "More pink" is not a new guide. A different hero product or a clearly distinct vibe counts.

## Step 2 — Reason through trends

Before generating ideas, think through what is trending RIGHT NOW across:

**Pinterest trends to consider:**
- Coquette / bow-core (ultra-feminine, bows, ribbons, blush, lace, pearl)
- Dark aesthetic gaming (deep green, forest academia, moody lighting, dark wood)
- Japandi / warm minimalist (Japanese + Scandinavian, linen, wood, neutral tones)
- Purple / lavender gaming (growing fast — lavender keyboards, purple lighting)
- Y2K / retro (holographic, chrome, nostalgic, CRT vibes)
- Cottagecore upgraded (mushroom accessories, botanical, earth tones)
- Cherry blossom / Sakura Japanese (pink meets Japanese minimal)
- Beige / cream aesthetic (neutral, linen, soft focus, adult-minimal)
- Soft goth / dark pastel (muted purples, black + pastel contrast)
- Sanrio / kawaii upgraded (building on pink flower guide — different anchor product)

**Filters — only suggest a trend if:**
- It has a distinct anchor product that can be the hero of the guide
- At least 8 shoppable, real Amazon products exist to fill the guide
- It appeals to the store's audience: women 18-34 who care about aesthetics
- It is clearly different from the four existing guides
- It has searchable Pinterest keywords with real volume


## Output rules

- Always generate at least 3 ideas, max 5
- Product names in the table can be generic descriptions (e.g. "lavender mechanical keyboard") — real ASINs come later when building the guide
- Do NOT invent fake trending aesthetics — only name trends that are real and verifiable
- Do NOT suggest guides that are too similar to existing ones (e.g. "light pink gaming setup" is too close to the pink cozy guide)
- Keep brand voice in the Vibe descriptions: warm, cozy, direct — not corporate
- If the user specifies a direction (e.g. "something darker", "more Y2K"), filter the output to trends that match
